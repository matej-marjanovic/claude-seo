## 3. 3-Month Execution Roadmap

### Month 1: Template & Foundation
*   **Design:** Build the `posthog.com/vs/[competitor]` programmatic template.
*   **Data Structure:** Map competitive features (e.g., pricing, self-hosting, plugins, session replay) to a central database.
*   **Pages:** Launch the top 3 direct competitors (Mixpanel, Amplitude, GA4).

### Month 2: Segment Expansion
*   **Content:** Expand the comparison pages to include niche and open-source competitors (LogRocket, Matomo, Plausible).
*   **Internal Linking:** Implement a "Comparison Hub" to cross-link these pages and boost their topical authority.

### Month 3: Optimization & Scale
*   **Conversion:** A/B test CTAs on the comparison pages (e.g., "Start for free" vs. "Book a demo").
*   **Expansion:** Add a secondary programmatic layer: `PostHog + [Platform Name]` (e.g., "PostHog + Next.js", "PostHog + Django") for technical integration keywords.
