### Google Images SEO notes

- Prefer **WebP** or **AVIF** with **explicit width/height** in HTML to protect CLS.
- Alt text should describe **what is in the image**, not keyword spam; one primary phrase is enough.
- Use **descriptive, hyphenated filenames**; keep them stable after indexation when possible.
