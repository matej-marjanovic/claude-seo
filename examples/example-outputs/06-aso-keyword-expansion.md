# Example 6: ASO keyword expansion (AllTrails)

## Original prompt

> Act as the seo-aso agent. Analyze the primary keyword 'hiking trail maps' for the iOS App Store and Google Play Store. Suggest an optimized App Title (max 30 characters), Subtitle, and a backend keyword list for the AllTrails app to maximize their visibility against competitors like Strava and Komoot.

## Generated output

### Store reality check

- **iOS App Store — title:** up to **30 characters** (prompt is correct).
- **Google Play — title:** up to **30 characters** as of recent policy emphasis on short titles; verify current Play Console limits when publishing.

### Suggested metadata (illustrative; validate against live brand guidelines)

**iOS — App Name (≤30 chars)**  
`AllTrails: Hike, Walk, Maps` (29 characters)  
*Alternative if “maps” must lead:* `AllTrails: Trail Maps` (21 chars) — leaves room for seasonal tests.

**iOS — Subtitle (≤30 chars)**  
`Hiking maps & GPS navigation` (28 characters)

**iOS — Keyword field (100 chars, comma-separated, no spaces after commas)**  
`hiking,trails,gps,walk,run,outdoor,topo,map,compass,offline,nationalpark,camping,backpacking`

**Google Play — Short description (80 chars)**  
`Hiking trail maps, GPS navigation, and offline topo maps for every adventure.` (77 chars)

**Google Play — Full description themes to hit**  
trail maps, offline maps, topo, national parks, GPS, hike planning, difficulty, photos, community reviews — **without keyword stuffing**.

### Competitor positioning (Strava / Komoot)

- **Strava** owns social + fitness tracking; AllTrails should own **trail discovery, conditions, and park coverage** in the title/subtitle, not “social network for athletes.”
- **Komoot** owns tour planning + routing; AllTrails should stress **North American trail coverage**, **park trailheads**, and **offline safety** where accurate.

### Backend / hidden keywords mindset (where the store allows)

- Play **does not** use a separate 100-char Apple-style keyword field; rely on **title, short description, long description**, and **proper categorization**.
- For Apple, avoid repeating words already in title/subtitle in the keyword field (waste of space).

*This example is strategic copy, not live App Store Connect / Play Console data.*
